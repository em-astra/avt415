P.P1.3: User Scenario Mapping

Scenario

Ava is a college student planning on throwing a birthday party next week because she is turning 20. She already has a dress picked out and is now looking for affordable but good quality jewelry to finalize her look. She is looking for a wide range of jewelry to pick from because she is unsure about what she is specifically looking for.

Actor

Ava.

Motivator

The motivator is Ava wants to look good for her birthday party.

Intention

She wants to find affordable and good quality jewelry to match the style of her dress.

Action

She looks online through various stores looking for styles of jewelry that match her fashion taste.

Resolution

She finds an online store that offers a variety of styles that she can mix and match with her dress and overall wardrobe. She orders a pair of earrings from the store and wears them at the party.
